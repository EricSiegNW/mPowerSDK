You can only share your coded study data. This is to protect your privacy.

Your coded study data does not include your name or information that directly identifies you. Instead of using your name, we use a random code to label your study data. This is your coded study data. Researchers will not be able to easily figure out your identity using your coded study data.

Sharable data are coded study data from:

  • Survey responses
  • Activity measurements
  • Comments you make through the app
  • Fitness tracker sensor data (if applicable)
  • Recordings or Photos taken within the app (if applicable)
